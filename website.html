++THE HOLLOW FORGE++

WHAT ARE FORGE WORLDS

Forge worlds are planets deemed fit by the Machine God to be cradles for His creations. They are planets blessed with industry, devoted eternity to toiling away to churn out the endless quantities of weapons, munitions, armor, vehicles, and other wargear. They are all utilized by the endless armies of the Imperium of man. These planets are beyond ancient, dating back to the Dark Age of Technology and surviving the Age of Strife more than 20 millennia ago. These factories cover the entirety of the planet's surface and can go kilometers deep through the crust to mine away at its resources. The tens of billions of souls who inhabit these planets have their bodies stripped down and rebuilt with cybernetics so that they may survive the toxic conditions of the heavily polluted planet. One of these planets, dear pilgrim, is the planet Lucius.

OUR DEFENSES

Our inexhaustable armies

The Skitarii are the cybernetic military forces of the Adeptus Mechanicus. They are uncountable faithful servants of the Machine God who are stationed to protect forge worlds. They were born human, but they were rid of the burden of their flesh and emotion; for flesh gets tired while the machine does not, for flesh feel fear while the machine does not. They are built to withstand the harshest environments. They will stand vigil without needing to sleep or rest for the Omnissiah drives them to greater heights.

Planetary Defense Force

The planet is covered with as much anti-voidship and anti-air weaponry as much as there are factories. These weapons represent the wrath of the Machine God to those who would harm its servants. They are built to fire the largest of shells. These weapons can be dozens of meteres long that can fire munitions are large as small vehicles or lasershots as strong as the heat of a small star. Once the planet becomes a target for the pitible forces of the enemy, the planet's rage will not cease until all foes are vanquished or ammunition has been completely depleted.

The Imperial Navy

No foe is safe from annihilation, even in the void of the stars. The holy forces of the Imperial Navy are outfitted with ships that are kilometers long, firing shells as large as train cars from Ancient Terra. These avatars of the Machine God's contempt for the heretic are floating cities in space as they are homes to hundreds of thousands of crew. They are holy relics that are thousands of years old, where generations of crew are born and died in its catacombs. They are armed with armor plates meters thick so that no weapon, save that from their own, can harm them.

WHY WE STAND OUT

What separates Lucius from the rest of the forge worlds is that we have access to something that even Mars, the birthplace of the Mechanicus, doesn't: the unmatched energy of a sun. Millenia ago, the people of Ancient Terra theorized how to capture the a sizable amount of energy of a star by emcompassing it in a megatructure. The entirety of the planet Lucius is one such megastructure. The energy output provides the forge world with more than enough power to meet its manufacturing needs. Lucius-pattern weapons and warengines have garnered a reputation for excellence across the Imperium. These weapons are made of Lucium, an alloy that can only be forged with the energy of a captured star. Those who walk with weapons made from Lucius are walking with relics as a holy warrior of the Machine God
